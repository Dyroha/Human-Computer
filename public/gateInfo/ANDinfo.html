The AND gate is the most simple gate of all (same as the demo)

It will only output a black flag '🏴' if both inputs are black flags '🏴'

If the inputs are different it will output a white flag '🏳️'

And finally if both inputs are white flags '🏳️' it will output a white flag '🏳️'
